About Skill.d

Skill.d was created by 5 STEM students from the University of North Carolina at Charllotte. The goal was to create a product that makes the job finding process easier and more efficient for other STEM students. Knowing the issues and struggles with the existing processes for job matching and applications, we were able to create a product that we belive fills in the gaps to make finding jobs that fit you and tracking the process of your applications much less frustrating.

Benefits of using Skill.d:

  1. Accurate job listing matches
  2. Tracking status of multiple applications
  3. Many job listing sites combined in one location
